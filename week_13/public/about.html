about

My name is Samantha Nissen and I currently live in Iowa City, IA. For the past two years, I have worked at the International Writing Program at the University of Iowa as the Coordinator for Outreach and Special Programs. This fall, I am relocating to Chicago for graduate school. As a front-end web developer, I love creating websites that are beautiful and accessible to all.

My other interests include intercultural communication, accessible education, and cultural exchange. Because of this, I enjoy working with clients who are working towards the greater good, including non-profit organizations, governmental agencies, and higher education institutions. If you would like to work together, email me at samanthaknissen@gmail.com.
